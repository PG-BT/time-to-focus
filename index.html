FAQ

Answers to common questions about Time To Focus.

How do I activate Premium?

After purchase, copy your license key and paste it in the extension settings.

Does it work without an account?

Yes. No account is required. Your rules and stats are saved locally in your browser.

Will it block on both Chrome and Edge?

Yes. It supports Chromium-based browsers like Chrome and Edge (and also Firefox).

What is “Full Focus” mode?

It’s a Pomodoro-style timer with stronger blocking so distractions can’t sneak in mid-session.

Why do I still see a site sometimes?

If the page was already open before a block started, refresh it. Also check exceptions / allow-lists and time rules.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: Dec 2025

1) Overview

We take privacy seriously. Time To Focus is built to help you focus without tracking you. We aim to minimize data collection and keep your information under your control.

2) Extension data (Chrome / Edge / Firefox)

The Time To Focus extension does not collect, store, or sell your personal data on our servers. Your rules, schedules, goals, and statistics are stored locally in your browser.

We do not collect browsing history, visited URLs, or the content of pages you browse.

3) Support & contact forms (Web3Forms)

If you contact us through the website contact form or a support form inside the app, we collect only the information you explicitly provide, such as your email address, selected topic, and message content.

In the extension support form, if you choose to enable Include debug info, the message may also include optional technical details (for example: browser type, language, timezone, and a timestamp) to help us troubleshoot faster.

Messages sent via the form are transmitted to our support inbox using a trusted third-party form provider (Web3Forms). This information is used solely to respond to your support request. We do not use support messages for marketing, advertising, or profiling.

4) Data retention

Support messages are retained only as long as necessary to resolve your request and for basic support record-keeping.

Disclaimer: “Boost focus by up to 2.5×” is based on self-reported user outcomes and may vary.
